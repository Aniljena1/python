PARAMETER VARIABLE
------------------
parameter is passed the data to a function.
Ex:
def addition(a,b,c,d):
    print("Addition of 4 intiger is=",a+b+c+d)
def subtraction(a,b,c,d,e):
    print("Subtraction of 4 intiger is=",a-b-c-d-e)

output
------
>>> addition(10,20,30,40)
Addition of 4 intiger is= 100
>>> subtraction(100,20,30,20,10)
Subtraction of 4 intiger is= 20
